Here is the functional design of a converter from number to string value. This converter transforms a ‘#’-separated list of numbers into their corresponding letter in the alphabet at that position (0 is converted to A, 1 converted to B, …). When arriving to 26, the letter comes back to A.

As an example, 0#1#2#26#27#52 is converted to ABCABA.

To enter values, the user must click on a keypad (no way to enter values manually).
